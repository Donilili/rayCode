- Mobile First Design -

Mobile-first design is a fundamental approach in web and app development that prioritizes creating a seamless and engaging user experience on mobile devices before considering larger screens. By starting with mobile as the primary focus, designers and developers ensure that their products are accessible and user-friendly on smartphones and tablets, which are the most common devices used to access digital content today.
